Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Command Golf ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Command Golf is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics services.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location or online activity.

Local Data Storage

The app stores your game progress (completed levels, star ratings) locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

Command Golf does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Command Golf is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
